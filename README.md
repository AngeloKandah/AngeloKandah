Phone Conversation

Sam:  'Cas, phone for you!'

Cas:  'Okay, thanks, Sam.'  --BEEP OF PHONE BEING TURNED ON--  'Hello?  Oh, hi....'

--AFTER PHONE CONVERSATION--

Sam:  'So, who was it?'

Cas:  'Heather.'

Sam:  'What did she want?'

Cas:  'Our phone number.'
